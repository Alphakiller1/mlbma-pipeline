# Chase Analytics — Marketing & Copy Guide

The canonical wording for the brand. Pull from here so every surface (site, OG tags, Discord,
Patreon, Instagram) says the same thing. When in doubt, shorter + concrete wins.

---

## One-line product description (canonical)
> **Chase Analytics — proprietary MLB offensive intelligence: daily matchups, team rankings, and player research, plus a private Discord.**

Short variant (bios, ≤ 80 chars):
> **Proprietary MLB matchup & offensive intelligence.**

## Landing-page headline options
1. **MLB Offensive Intelligence** *(current — clean, on-brand)*
2. **The MLB offensive edge, quantified.**
3. **Matchups, rankings, and player research — built on proprietary metrics.**

Sub-headline:
> Daily matchup signals, team rankings, and pitcher/batter research — refreshed every morning.

## CTA wording (use these exact labels everywhere)
| Action | Label |
|---|---|
| Create account | **Sign up free** |
| Sign in | **Sign in** |
| Discord community (everyone) | **Join our Discord** |
| Premium membership | **Join Premium on Patreon** |
| Link Discord account (signed-in) | **Connect Discord** |
| Explore product | **Open the dashboard** |

Keep CTAs verb-first and consistent. Don't mix "Get started / Subscribe / Become a member /
Upgrade" for the same action — pick the label above.

## Discord wording
- Invite line: **"Join our Discord — daily signals, matchup talk, and model breakdowns."**
- Premium perk: **"Premium members get the private channels + the paid role, auto-assigned via Patreon."**
- Channel tone: concise, analytical, no hype. Always tag posts as research.

## Patreon wording
- Tier name: **Premium**
- Pitch: **"The full Chase Analytics edge — daily signals, the complete dashboard, and the private Premium Discord."**
- Always: **"Cancel anytime."** and **"For research & entertainment — bet responsibly."**

## Instagram bio / caption tone
- **Bio:** `Proprietary MLB offensive intelligence ⚾ Daily matchups, rankings & research → chase-analytics.com`
- **Caption tone:** confident but factual; lead with the insight, not hype. 1–2 short lines +
  3–6 targeted hashtags. Always close with a soft disclaimer.
- **Hashtags (rotate):** `#MLB #MLBAnalytics #SportsBetting #Baseball #MLBPicks #DFS #Sabermetrics`
- **Disclaimer (every post):** `Model-generated research. Not betting advice. 21+. Bet responsibly.`

## Forbidden / confusing phrases — avoid
- ❌ "Guaranteed," "locks," "sure thing," "free money," "can't lose" — false + risky.
- ❌ "Picks" as a *promise* of outcomes — we sell **research/signals**, not guaranteed picks.
- ❌ Mixing product names ("MLBMA" internal vs "Chase Analytics" public) — **public-facing is
  always "Chase Analytics."** "MLBMA" stays internal (code/data only).
- ❌ Inconsistent domain forms — always `chase-analytics.com` (hyphen, apex).
- ❌ Multiple CTA verbs for one action (see CTA table).

## Short feature descriptions (reusable blurbs)
- **Daily MLB matchup dashboard** — Every game's offensive matchup, lineup edges, weather/park
  context, and model signals, refreshed each morning.
- **Team rankings** — League-wide offensive rankings on proprietary metrics (OSI and more),
  with recent-form (L14) movement.
- **Pitcher & batter profiles** — Deep player pages: splits, percentile-vs-league bars, pitch
  mix, and matchup-adjusted projections.
- **Bullpen intel** — Unit and individual reliever profiles, usage, and strength-of-schedule
  context to flag exposed pens.
- **Research Lab / signals** — Daily convergence plays and model signals that surface where
  multiple indicators agree.

> Naming note: **"OSI"** = the proprietary Offensive Strength metric. Reference it as a
> proprietary metric; don't over-explain the formula in marketing.
