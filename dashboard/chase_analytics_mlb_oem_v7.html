Opponent Context
OOR ? Opponent Offensive Rating
How dangerous is the offense each team's pitching staff faces? Sorted by HvP (composite threat). Red = toughest to face, Green = softest schedule.
OOR = 55% HvR + 45% HvL
HvP = composite opponent threat
vP% = vs league average
# Team HvP HvL HvR vP% vP Rank vL Rank vR Rank

How to use OOR: Teams at the top face the toughest offenses ? their pitching stats may be suppressed by schedule. Teams at the bottom face the softest lineups ? their ERAs may be inflated by weak competition. Cross-reference with Pitching Score for a complete picture of pitching value vs. schedule context.

PALS ? Pitching-Adjusted Lineup Score
OSI adjusted for opposing SP quality faced this season. Higher = genuinely strong offense.
# Team OSI Avg xFIP Faced BA++ PTF+ PALS
Loading PALS data?
Method: BA++ = normalized OSI. PTF+ = normalized average xFIP of opposing starters faced (inverted ? higher values mean an easier SP schedule). PALS = average of BA++ and PTF+. Avg xFIP faced coloring: green above 4.05 (softer pitching mix), red below 3.95 (tougher); lower raw xFIP = harder schedule. PALS coloring: 70+ elite (green), 55?69 strong (gold), 40?54 neutral (text), under 40 weak (red).
